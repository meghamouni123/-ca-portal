How it works

From 10,000 news articles
to 50 exam-ready facts

Our pipeline runs every 30 minutes, fetching, filtering, deduplicating and summarizing — so you only read what matters.

📡
01

Fetch

RSS feeds from 170+ sources — The Hindu, PIB, Indian Express, ISRO, RBI & more — scraped every 30 min.

🧠
02

Classify

MPNet embeddings + Logistic Regression filter only exam-relevant news with ≥80% confidence. NOT_RELEVANT is discarded.

🔍
03

Deduplicate

Cosine similarity on embeddings removes cross-published duplicates. Same story from 5 sources → stored once.

04

Summarize

BART-large-CNN generates a clear paragraph summary. LexRank handles long government reports gracefully.
